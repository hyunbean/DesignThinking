# [P2] 컨셉 일러스트 — 영문 이미지 생성 프롬프트 2종

**입력**: [P1]의 "컨셉 스케치" 필드
**산출 형태**: 이미지 생성 도구 미연동 환경이므로, 외부 이미지 모델에 붙여넣을 완성형 영문 프롬프트 2건을 산출.

---

## 1. 제품/컨셉 일러스트 (구조·작동 방식)

```
Hand-drawn concept sketch on a clean whiteboard, black marker line art,
workshop style, NOT photorealistic. Monochrome with orange accent color only
on key elements and flow arrows.

A large smartphone screen mockup: a cute baby fox standing on a grassy field
inside the app. Top of screen: goal card labeled "JEJU TRIP 400,000 KRW" with
a progress bar at 62% (orange fill). Beside the fox, a piggy bank with a coin
dropping in; the fox jumps happily. Bottom row: five growth-stage icons EGG →
BABY → KID → ADULT → FINAL with "KID" lit up in orange.

Around the phone, sketch-style system diagram arrows: a bank icon labeled
"OPEN BANKING" with an arrow "AUTO-DETECT SAVING" into the phone, and an arrow
from a food-delivery icon labeled "OVERSPENDING?" leading to a small sad fox
face labeled "HUNGRY PET ALERT". Short English labels only, simple and clear.
```

## 2. 사용 장면 일러스트 (실사용 맥락)

```
Hand-drawn concept sketch on a clean whiteboard, black marker line art,
workshop style, NOT photorealistic. Monochrome with orange accent color used
sparingly.

Scene: a college student sitting on a dorm bed at night, holding a smartphone,
a food delivery app visible on a second thought bubble being crossed out. On
the phone screen, the fully evolved fox stands under fireworks with a banner
"GOAL!" and a collection shelf below showing two previously raised animals
with medals. The student smiles; a speech bubble says "SAVED IT!". An orange
arrow connects the crossed-out delivery food thought to the growing pet,
labeled "SKIP → FEED YOUR PET". Simple storyboard framing, short English
labels only.
```
